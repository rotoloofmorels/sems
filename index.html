Satellite-based Environmental Monitoring.

We built a highly advanced shadow removal algorithm for high accuracy boundary detection to monitor land use change via satellite imaging, and flag activities disturbing ecological balance.

Launch

Detection.

Our model is a deep encoder-decoder architecture for multi-class pixelwise segmentation, trained specifically to detect vegetation, and barren areas.

Accuracy.

We built the most advanced shadow removal algorithm for high accuracy boundary detection.

Realtime monitoring.

Our model can check for forest encroachment, lake bed encroachment, pollution levels in the lake and river bodies and also point out the areas causing them.

Advantage.

The cost of monitoring via a satellite mechanism is 1/20th the cost of monitoring using human resources and is 3 times more accurate.

Make the difference.

Our project makes a difference, aiding local and central governments in monitoring land use seamlessly, catalysing a closed-loop monitoring mechanism for pollution

Extensions.

Our system can extend to various use cases, such as pollution measurement using reflectance pattern, satellite based monitoring of illegal construction activities, as well as disaster management and mitigation by real time monitoring.
